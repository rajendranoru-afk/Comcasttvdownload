How to Download the Comcast Xfinity TV App on Android

If you want the Comcast TV App for Android, follow these steps:

  1. Open the app store available on your device.
  2. Search for “Xfinity Stream App” or “Comcast TV App.”
  3. Select the official app from Comcast.
  4. Click on the download or install option.
  5. Open the app once installed and sign in with your Comcast Xfinity account.

How to Download the Xfinity TV App on iPhone or iPad

Apple users can download the Xfinity TV App for iOS through the official app store. Follow these steps:

  1. Open the app store on your iPhone or iPad.
  2. Type “Comcast TV App” or “Xfinity Stream App” in the search box.
  3. Select the official Comcast application.
  4. Press the “Get” button to start the download.
  5. After installation, launch the app and log in using your Comcast account.
